# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** small-to-mid-size business owners, startup founders, and agency leads who
need a Laravel / React / WordPress developer but don't have in-house engineering judgment
to vet one. They arrive skeptical — hiring an unfamiliar freelancer is risky — and
time-poor, often on mobile from a search result or referral, scanning for proof before
they'll commit to reaching out.

**Secondary:** recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Ohene for full-time roles.

**Job to be done:** "Convince me within one scroll that you can solve my problem, show me
evidence that de-risks hiring you, then make it trivial to get in touch."

## Product Purpose

A portfolio + client-acquisition site whose primary job is **business development, not
resume display**. It exists to:

1. Pitch Ohene's ability to ship production software a prospective client can trust.
2. Provide evidence — quantified case studies, testimonials, writing — that reduces the
   risk of hiring an unfamiliar freelancer.
3. Capture and track leads instead of losing them to a generic mailbox.
4. Build long-term organic search visibility through indexable case studies and a blog.

Success looks like qualified inbound and growing organic reach: ≥5 qualified leads/month
and +50% organic sessions by six months post-launch.

## Brand Personality

**Confident craftsman.** Direct, plain-language voice — no jargon for its own sake.
Confidence is backed by specifics (numbers, named technologies, quantified outcomes),
never adjectives. Three words: **technical, accountable, proof-driven**. The signature
visual language — terminal/file-path eyebrows (`~/work`), the Kente-weave/circuit
divider, monospaced stats — signals "a developer who sweats the details" without
shouting. The feeling a prospect should leave with: *this person ships production
software and stays accountable after launch.*

## Anti-references

- **Generic template / off-the-shelf portfolio** (ThemeForest, Wix) that could belong to
  anyone. The identity must be specific and self-built — the site is itself a work sample.
- **Corporate / agency stock look** — stock-photo teams, buzzword soup, faceless-
  consultancy polish. This is one accountable person with a real track record; keep it
  personal and concrete.
- **Cliché SaaS-landing scaffolding** — gradient hero + identical feature-card grid + a
  big-number metric row as the whole story. Evidence carries the page, not decoration.

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over claims** — every section earns trust with specifics (quantified impact,
   named stack, real client work), not adjectives.
2. **One scroll to conviction** — the pitch and evidence a skeptical buyer needs are
   reachable fast; each page has exactly one clear primary CTA (Start a project / Contact).
3. **Practice what you preach** — the site itself is the portfolio piece: fast
   (LCP < 2.5s), accessible, server-rendered, secure. Built on the stack Ohene sells.
4. **Developer craft as identity** — the terminal/Kente motif is a deliberate signature
   used with restraint: distinctive, never decoration for its own sake.
5. **Every visitor is a potential lead** — minimise friction to contact, capture the
   source/context of each inquiry, and never lose one to a black-hole inbox.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target: ≥4.5:1 contrast on body text across every template; all interactive
elements keyboard-navigable with visible focus states; images require alt text (enforced
at the CMS level for cover images). `prefers-reduced-motion` is respected for scroll-reveal
and hover motion. Tested mobile-first at 375 / 768 / 1280 breakpoints.
