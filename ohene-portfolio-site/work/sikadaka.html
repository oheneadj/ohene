Sikadaka

A Laravel-based financial management system for community savings groups.

sikadaka.app
Challenge

Informal savings communities needed a transparent way to register members and track contributions without manual ledgers.

Build

A Laravel-based system for creating communities, registering members and tracking payments, engineered to scale to 1,000+ members.

Impact

Gives administrators full transaction visibility and financial transparency across the community.
